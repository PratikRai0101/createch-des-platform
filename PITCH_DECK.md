## Slide 2: The Core Problem
*   **Headline:** The Cost of Reactive Construction
*   **Bullet Points:**
    *   **The Disconnect:** BIM models are static. Site realities are dynamic. 
    *   **The Bottleneck:** When site conditions deviate (e.g., poor soil bearing capacity, structural out-of-plumb), work stops. 
    *   **The Impact:** Data is sent back to engineering, taking **weeks** for a redesign. Millions of dollars are lost in idle machinery and delayed schedules.
*   **Visual Suggestion:** A timeline showing the traditional "Stop Work -> Send Data -> Wait for Redesign -> Resume" vs. our proposed instant cycle.
*   **Speaker Notes:** "In heavy engineering, the biggest capital bleeds don't happen in planning; they happen during execution. When site conditions change, traditional workflows require a full stop. We wanted to reduce that redesign turnaround from 3 weeks to 300 milliseconds."

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution - The Digital Execution System
*   **Headline:** A Closed-Loop Autonomous Platform
*   **Bullet Points:**
    *   **1. Sense (Edge IoT & CV):** Real-time YOLO tracking and geotechnical sensors monitor the site 24/7.
    *   **2. Analyze (Digital Twin):** Sensor data is mapped instantly to a React Three Fiber 3D interactive model.
    *   **3. Adapt (Generative AI):** A Python FastAPI ML engine recalculates structural topologies on the fly when constraints break.
*   **Visual Suggestion:** A circular flywheel diagram connecting [IoT/Cameras] -> [Digital Twin] -> [Generative Design] -> back to [Execution].
*   **Speaker Notes:** "We built a platform that doesn't just display dashboards. It acts. It senses reality, maps it to a digital twin, and autonomously generates new engineering designs if the physical site deviates from the plan."
